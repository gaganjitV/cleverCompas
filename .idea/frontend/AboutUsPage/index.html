Welcome to Clever Compass!
Clever Compass, developed by the Crypto Knights team at Sacramento State University, is an innovative platform designed to enhance the academic journey for students while providing efficient tools for tutors. Guided by Professor Maryam Siddique, Clever Compass aims to improve operational efficiency, track tutor performance, and offer a streamlined learning experience .
